3. Discussion

We can get the results from the visualization clearly and vividly. I mapped the occurrence of an earthquake to a visual mark represented as a dot. And data attributes such as frequency will be mapped to the color of dot with a exact number.

Utilizing a map to show the information highly related to geography is a intuitive and efficient way. Interactive information query method is more conducive to the interest and simplicity of information query.

As for the weakness of this approach, it is clearly lacking in aggregate statistics. It is difficult to summarize a certain statistical index in different dimensions. For example, I want to clearly compare the top 10 countries with the most earthquakes in the last decade, or I want to calculate the change in earthquake frequency around the world.
